## Core Objective: Analogical Transfer

**One of the fundamental goals of this system is the ability to project a known path into a new set of concepts and re-use that pattern to tackle those new concepts.**

For example: knowing how to bake an apple pie should still apply to a pear pie, or even bread, or a quiche. The system should even be able to apply culinary patterns to something farfetched, like lighting a fire and cooking something on an open fire ‚Äî the underlying structure of "prepare ingredients ‚Üí combine ‚Üí apply heat ‚Üí achieve transformation" transfers across domains.

This is **learning as geometry**: when you traverse a path through one region of the manifold (apple ‚Üí pie), that path's structure (the sequence of steps, the curvature that formed) can be projected onto analogous regions (pear, bread, open fire cooking) because the geometric relationships encode the abstract pattern, not just the specific instances.

### ‚öõÔ∏è 1Ô∏è‚É£ The "Virtual Stage" as Cognitive Physics
